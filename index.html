About Us

 Welcome to Sustain You, a digital haven committed to curating a diverse collection of smaller businesses and ethical, environmentally sustainable brands all in one place! At Sustain You, we believe in the power of conscious consumerism, and our mission is to make it easier for you to discover, support, and connect with businesses that prioritize ethical practices and environmental sustainability.

Our website is a carefully curated marketplace designed to cater to those seeking unique, long-lasting, and transparently sourced products for their everyday lives. Whether you are passionate about reducing your carbon footprint, supporting fair labor practices, or simply looking for high-quality items with a story to tell, Sustain You is your go-to destination.

Our Mission

As fashion is a global phenomenon, it is important to be aware of the environmental impacts of our clothing choices. Fast fashion is a popular type of fashion industry that is driven by a desire to keep up with the latest trends and styles. Although fast fashion provides many with cheap options to be able to follow what is trending, studies shown that these fast fashion industries are not only harmful to the environment, but also have nonethical practices. It is known that some fast fashion brands pay their sweatshop workers very low wages in unsafe conditions. Additionally, they may not be transparent with where they source their materials from on their websites.

With Sustain Us, we want to be able to redirect people away from fast fashion and educate them on the environmental impacts of fast fashion. We provide a variety of options listing clean and ethical brands, ranging from clothing brands to accessories. We also provide a listing of events and flea markets that are happening in NYC inclusively that are also ethically sourced and not harmful to our environment.
